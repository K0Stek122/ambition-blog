# About Me
My name is Kam. At the time of writing this I am 21 years old and I have graduated Computer Science at University College Birmingham, and I am moving on to a master's degree at University of Nottingham in Cyber Physical Systems and Cyber Security.

## Why this blog
It's so easy to go down the productivity loophole, the purpose of this blog is to simplify this spiralling environment.

I want to provide swift and quick advice that anyone can follow. I want to stay simple and minimalistic. I move away from heavy and money-intensive approaches and I prioritise simple systems that follow KISS principles (Keep it simple, stupid).
